If you are interested in listing an investment opportunity for sale we will first meet with you at the property at your next available convenience. We will perform an inspection of your property and discuss the complete sales process with you. We will then put your property in our investment engine to recommend actions and a price point to place you in the best position to sell your property. You will also be provided with a thorough comparative market analysis so you can see the sales price of similar properties in your area over the past 3 years. We will market your property for sale in the multiple listing service, giving thousands of agents the ability to view your property and present it to your seller as well as promoting it as a featured listing on our website. Once your property is under contract, we will help to make sure that everything goes smoothly until the date of closing.
